Overview:

Kigali Allied Advocates (KAA) is a corporate and commercial law firm that focuses its practice in providing quality and solution oriented services. With the diverse knowledge, professional qualification and experience of members of KAA Law team, KAA constantly endeavours to provide solution oriented legal services.

KAA has a good blend of Rwanda and international clients, commercial and not-for-profit organizations. As part of discharging its professional and societal responsibility, KAA renders pro-bono services. Members of KAA Law team devote a part of their time and efforts to specific focused areas of practice and actively participate in professional, industry and academic activities.

KAA focuses its practice in its specialized domain of corporate and commercial laws including dispute resolution and litigation.

KAA has strong presence in the areas of mergers and acquisitions, combinations, competition and anti-trust, joint ventures and collaborations, corporate and business structuring & restructuring, capital raising, legal due diligence, legal and regulatory audit, securities law, employment laws, corporate and tax litigation, litigation in general and arbitration.
